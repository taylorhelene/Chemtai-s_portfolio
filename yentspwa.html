Fintech app design

Introduction

  • Category: App design
  • Client: Yen Africa
  • Project date: February, 2021

Yen progressive web app is the result of an end-to-end user experience project I undertook as a software designer and developer at Yen Africa.This study follows my journey from identifying a problem space, learning about the problem, and creating a solution.
The problem: Many golfers within the club are usually interested in cryptocurrency and they would like a way to invest their money in a coin that would grow in value and earn them some interest while being a part of the golfing club.


Solution: Yen progressive web app that enables golfers to transact and gain interest on their money while being able to conduct payments for their various golfing activities.
My Role: As a UI/UX team of four I was responsible for the :

My Process

My process employed a design thinking mindset, in which I focused first on learning about the problem space, through the discovery stages of understanding and empathy, then synthesizing what I had learned to define and frame the problem and finally creating the solution.
